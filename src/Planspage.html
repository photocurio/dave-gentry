What you get:

Plans - Boat plans include a comprehensive, well illustrated building guide, material and tool requirements and full sized patterns for most every part. The construction guide includes complete instructions for building the frame, skinning the hull and coating the fabric - as well as sources for fabric and hardware. We want you succeed, and the plans reflect that.

Measurements are in both English and metric.

Instant downloads are now available for all of our designs. Electronic plans are $10 to $20USD less than paper plans, there is no shipping charge, and they arrive right away. The caveat is that you will have to have the file of templates printed out on a large format printer - which most every local print shop will have - for a nominal fee.

All plan sets include full sized templates for most all the plywood parts - frames, stems, transoms, knees, skeg, etc. Just glue to a sheet of plywood and saw along the lines - no lofting required, and no worries about having misread a bunch of measurements.

Frame Kits

Frame kits include the building guide, pre-cut frames, transoms, knees, etc, and all the fabric needed to skin your boat. Due to shipping costs and difficulties kits do not include the longitudinal stringers (Gunwales, chines, keel, et al.), or spars, nor epoxy or fasteners, paint, etc. Sailboat kits do include wood for the foils, but do not include sails.

Some kits have optional components, so all kits are ordered directly. Send an email to GentryCustomBoats@yahoo.com and we will send you a paypal invoice. Turnaround time for kits is usually under a week. Shipping is usually less than $50 in the USA.

Note: We use only BS1088 Okoume marine plywood in our kits - the best, by far.
